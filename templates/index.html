A new method of storing medical records and getting prompt assistance.

A movement to increase efficiency and improve the logistics of hospitals around the globe.


An easy method to store important medical data and access it anywhere.


Current methods of storing medical records and other valuable information are incredibly inefficient. Our service attempts to deliver a concise way of viewing medical records. In addition, the website is updated with a live map able to tell you where the closest hospitals are with information on closing and opening times.



Features and Usage


-Google Maps embed provides directions and gives a sense of the closest emergency room locations
-Patients can easily view and store medical records and transport them from hospital to hospital easily
-Administrators can update one's vaccination records and more without having to worry about storing papers or losing them
